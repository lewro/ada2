About the Course

Our application design course teaches all the skills you need to become a professional in this field. Instructor Roman Leinwather has learned and has been using these skills during the last 15 years and these are the exact skills he uses every day when working for clients from around the world.

The course consists of a practical, systematic, step-by-step approach teaching everything from design basics, using industry-standard tool Adobe Xd, creating wireframes, user flows, interactive prototypes and final design mockups.

What if you need help? You are entitled to two hours of reply time. You can ask questions directly your instructor by use of our contact form.
